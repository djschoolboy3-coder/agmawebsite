Why sell with Gunderson

The benefits of a well-engineered exit.

A sale isn't just a payday — done right, it's the highest-leverage financial event of your career. Here's what selling the Gunderson way actually gets you.

Liquidity now

Turn years of equity into cash — through an all-cash exit or structured financing — instead of waiting and hoping the market stays kind.

A higher multiple

The readiness program

We make you sellable — before you go to market.

Most agencies aren't immediately sale-ready, and that's fine. Rather than turn you away, we engineer the value up first. Every lever below is something a buyer prices — and something we improve.

  • Reduce owner-dependency — so the buyer isn't buying your calendar.
  • Stabilize recurring revenue — convert project work into retainers wherever possible.
  • Document fulfillment — systems, SOPs, and a team that operates without you.
  • Diversify client concentration — so no single account is the deal-killer in diligence.
  • Clean up financials — defensible recasts, accurate add-backs, audit-ready books.
